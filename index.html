About Me

I am currently a student in the coding bootcamp at Columbia University. I have no prior experience in computer programming so I am very excited to embark on this new adventure! I am very interested in the details of learning codes and it's functions, along with the creative aspect of it as well. The class is very challenging and there is a lot of material we are covering. However, I know with hard work and perseverance you can achieve anything! I danced for the NFL as a New York Jets Cheerleader after many years of auditioning. I trained hard and never gave up, even when I felt like I was out of my league. So I look forward to see where coding takes me in the pursuit of finding a more fulfilling and meaningful career!

When I'm not learning to code, I enjoy movies, Bikram yoga and spending time with my family which consists of my boyfriend, Jesse and our Mini Golden Retriever, Milo. People often say, "I didn't know they made mini golden retrievers!" Well we did not either! They are mixed with cocker spaniels and that's how they stay small. Milo is full grown at 30lb and is the perfect NYC apartment size pup! It will be nice someday to have a backyard for Milo to run in. I have been living in New York for 10 years now and I hope to make it back out to Seattle where I am originally from.
